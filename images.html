This does yet not work in Internet Explorer. Please try Firefox or Chrome.

Drag-and-drop image files (JPEG, GIF, PNG) from your computer below. You'll need at least 25 images to fill all the squares. Small images (try 128x128 or 150x150) that are approximately the same size work best.

When ready, click the Generate Bingo Card button. Currently this page creates just one bingo card at a time, but you can click the Generate Bingo Card button again to create more random cards with the same images.
